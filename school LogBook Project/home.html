Welcome to College Logbook

Welcome to BMS (Best Management System)
Our school logbook is a web application where students and teachers can keep track of their
academic progress. It allows users to register and login to access different features such as
managing semesters, subjects, and teachers.
Here you can find the list of all the teachers, semesters and register for your courses.
Our system is very user friendly and easy to navigate.
We have a lot of feature like searching, editing and so on.
Feel free to explore and enjoy your stay.

About Us

This application was developed as a part of a school project. It is designed to help students and teachers keep track of their academic progress.
